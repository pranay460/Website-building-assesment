Maunga Club Chalets

Chalet Kakapo

Chalet 1 - Max Adults 10 - Max Children 30

Kakapo is our largest chalet. It sports a massive kitchen, dining and living area with great amenities.

Chalet Pukeko

Chalet 2 - Max Adults 6 - Max Children 15

Pukeko is great for large groups or families wanting to book out a chalet all to themselves. It's very roomy. Occupancy is deliberately limited to allow for that extra comfortable stay.

Chalet Kereru

Chalet 3 - Max Adults 2 - Max Children 4

Kereru is the perfect getaway for the family. Sporting 2 bedrooms, it's specifically tailored to families with 2 to 4 children. Extra spaces in the living area for kids or parents to escape to.
